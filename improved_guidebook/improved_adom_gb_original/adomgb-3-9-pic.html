The right side of the level is random.

The temple layout and trap locations are guaranteed. The trap types
are not guaranteed. The type of trap is dependent on the dungeon
level where the elemental temple is generated. The traps displayed
here are observed when the Water Temple is generated on D: 19.

The monsters are:

s - Snake from Beyond
C - chaos servants
s - water snakes
& - water demons
x - water grues
E - water elementals
