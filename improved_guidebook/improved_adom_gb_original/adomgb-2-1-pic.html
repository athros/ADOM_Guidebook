The sealed room contains a cursed scroll of chaos resistance and a strange message.
The potion below the stairs on the left is a potion of ultra healing.

The monsters are:

@ - Gaab'Baay, the old crone (wanders after secret door is discovered)
@ - Hawkslayer, the burly adventurer
@ - Leggot, the shopkeeper (always in his shop)
@ - Malicious Doctor (wanders if door is open)
@ - Oracle (never moves)
t - farmer (commonly generated but not guaranteed; location random)
O - Ogre Guardian (never moves)
r - ratling guardians (never move)

The High Mountain Village will also contain random monsters, some of which may well be hostile.

The #s are secret doors.
